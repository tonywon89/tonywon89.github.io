ABOUT MYSELF

My name is Tony Won and I am a software developer. But I didn't start out that way. In fact, I spent most of my life pursuing a different career path: veterinary medicine. I even got into the top veterinary school at the time, Cornell University, and attended for two years (out of four) before I changed paths. Since then, I have never looked back, and couldn't be happier.


I first discovered the joy of creating things through playing Minecraft, where I spent hours building grand castles and advanced mining mechanisms After that, I wanted to translate this new found passion into something more practical, so I spent a summer learning computer programming through various online courses, such as Codecademy and Standford's CS106A: Programming Methodology. I built an Android app (which I still have on my phone to this day). After that, there was Harvard's CS50: Intro to Computer Science. After doing Michael Hartl's "Ruby on Rails Tutorial", I built my first full-stack web application: WorkToRelax. I have been hooked to coding since.


To turn coding into a career, I attended App Academy, a 1000-hour intensive web development course. It was an awesome experience! And now, I am aiming for a job at a company where I can not only contribute my knowledge, but also learn and grow as a developer.
